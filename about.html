About us

Haltom Logistics is a global leader in international transport & logistics. The mission of the company is to support its customers as they grow by providing customized service that allows them to be more competitive in their respective markets. Haltom Logistics is committed to delivering reliable, flexible innovative and value-creating solutions. Each of its strategic drivers is related to a commitment to its customers. Haltom Transport & Logistics is a major international transport and logistics operator with 35,000 employees across 109 countries on 5 continents that are working together to satisfy our global customers. Specializing in multimodal transport, the group is active in 4 businesses: Haltom Ports, Haltom Logistics, Haltom Energy and Haltom Railways.
